## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is an RS-232 modem?  
An RS-232 modem is a device that converts standard RS-232 serial data signals for transmission over fiber optic cable to support long-distance, interference-free communication.

### What types of systems use RS-232 modems?  
Systems such as industrial controllers, PLCs, test equipment, and legacy serial devices use RS-232 modems to communicate over extended distances.

### Is special configuration required?  
No most Versitron RS-232 modem solutions are plug-and-play and require minimal configuration.
